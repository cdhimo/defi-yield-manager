Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is LoopFi?

LoopFi uses bank-level security with smart contracts on the Solana blockchain. Your funds are always under your control, and we never have access to your private keys.

What are the minimum deposit requirements?

There's no minimum deposit for the Starter plan. Premium plans require a minimum of $100 to start earning optimal yields.

Can I withdraw my funds anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw your funds at any time. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours, though most are instant.

What happens if a bill payment fails?

We'll notify you immediately and retry the payment. If it continues to fail, we'll pause automated payments until you resolve the issue.

Are there any fees?

Starter plan is completely free. Premium plans have a monthly subscription fee, but no hidden fees on transactions or yields.

Which bills can I connect?

You can connect virtually any recurring payment including utilities, rent, subscriptions, credit cards, and loan payments.
