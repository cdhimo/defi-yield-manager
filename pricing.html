Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. We only charge the monthly subscription fee for Premium and Enterprise plans. All yield earnings are yours to keep.

What happens if I cancel?

You can cancel anytime. Your funds remain in your account, but automated features will be disabled. You can withdraw your funds at any time.

When will LoopFi launch?

We're currently in development and plan to launch in Q2 2024. Sign up for early access to be notified when we go live!
