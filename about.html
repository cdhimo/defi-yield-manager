About LoopFi

Revolutionizing how you manage expenses and earn passive income

Our Mission

LoopFi was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to turn your monthly expenses into a source of passive income. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to make their money work harder, even while paying bills.

What We Do

LoopFi automates your bill payments while simultaneously earning yield on your deposited funds. Instead of letting your money sit idle between bill payments, we put it to work in carefully selected DeFi yield strategies on the Solana blockchain.

Why Solana?

We chose Solana for its fast transaction speeds, low fees, and robust DeFi ecosystem. This allows us to offer competitive yields while maintaining the security and efficiency our users expect.
